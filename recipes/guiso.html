Guiso!

One of my favorite foods to find ready when I came back home from school for lunch; Guiso! In spanish this dish is actually called stew but I grew up and know it as guiso. This delicious plate is served while hot for those cold autumn and winter days, when some neccesary heat is needed. Down below, I will leave the ingredients along with the recipe! Enjoy!
