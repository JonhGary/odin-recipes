Guiso!

One of my favorite foods to find ready when I came back home from school for lunch; Guiso! In spanish this dish is actually called stew but I grew up and know it as guiso. This delicious plate is served while hot for those cold autumn and winter days, when some neccesary heat is needed. Down below, I will leave the ingredients along with the recipe! Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 8 pieces of chicken breast
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 can of puree tomato
  • 1 spanish caldo of chicken and vegetables
  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 can of mixed vegetables
  • Salt, oregano, paprika and

Now, time for the recipe!

  1. Cook the chicken with some oil. Cook until golden and then take it out.
  2. Dice the onions into little pieces and cook them in the same pot as the chicken.
  3. Add the pureed tomato can along with the tomato, season at your taste, add some sugar and half a liter of water. Put the chicken back in and cook for another 20 minutes at medium.
  4. After, put in the rice, caldo and cook until the rice is cooked. While you cook, you must add water to prevent the food from getting dry.
  5. Finally, add the vegetables and season again with salt and let it rest for a while!
  6. Serve and Bon-Apetit!
