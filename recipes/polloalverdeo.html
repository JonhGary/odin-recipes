Sauced Baked Chicken!

A delicious and easy meal cooked with a delicious sauce to go along with the chicken. This meal is meant to be eaten before the "siesta". It's supposed to help you sleep and feel well rested after this meal. Another one of my favorites, I'm excited to share it with everybody so here it goes!!

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • cooking wine
  • onions, green onions,parsley, garlic
  • heavy whipping cream
  • Salt, pepper, paprika,onion and garlic powder
  • rice on the side

Recipe time!!

  1. First, we dice the onions and the garlic and cook them in a pan with oil until they turn golden.
  2. Then we must season the chicken with all of the seasoning listed in the ingredients list.
  3. when the vegetables are golden, we add the chicken and let it cook for a few minutes.
  4. After both sides are semi cooked, we add some white wine and turn the heat on high
  5. We let the alcohol evaporate for a few minutes then add water enough to cover the chicken.
  6. Once the water starts boiling, we let it cook for a few more minutes and turn the heat back to medium.
  7. We then remove the pan from the stove and add the whipping cream and stur until the liquid becomes creamy
  8. You can then add raw green onion and parsley and serve with rice or mashed potatoes on the side! Enjoy!!
