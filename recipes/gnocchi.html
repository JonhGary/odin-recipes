Gnocchi (or Ã‘oquis)!

A fine Italian pasta dish! Another one of my favorites that my mom would routinely make every 29th of each month- as is custom in Argentina. This food is usually made with bolognese tomato sauce. A small plate of gnocchi would leave you extrememly full. A full stomach is a happy stomach! Check out the ingredients and the recipe down below! Try it out!

Ingredients

For 4 people:
  • Get a pack of gnocchi from a store which should be about 320 grams of pre made gnocchi.
  • Water
  • Basil
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • vegetables: onion, green pepper, red pepper, garlic
  • Seasoning: Paprika, Chili Powder, Salt, Pepper, Onion and Garlic Powder

Aaaaaand... here's the recipe!

  1. First we dice half an onion and garlic and cook it until its golden.
  2. We then add the ground beef and season it with salt, paprika, onion and garlic powder, chili powder and salt
  3. we cook the ground beef until most of it gains color and then we dice up the green and red pepper and put it in the pan.
  4. We add the tomato sauce along with half a cup of water.
  5. While we wait for the sauce to cook, we put a pot of water to boil at high heat for the gnocchi.
  6. Wait until the water boils and check on the sauce, make sure the sauce is cooked.
  7. When the water boils, add the gnocchi and wait until they start to raise to the top of the pot. Then you'll know that it's done.
  8. Now it's done! Empty the water and serve the gnocchi first and then add the sauce. Add some basil and some parmesan cheese if you'd like and enjoy!
