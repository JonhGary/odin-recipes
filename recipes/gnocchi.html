A fine Italian pasta dish! Another one of my favorites that my mom would routinely make every 29th of each month- as is custom in Argentina. This food is usually made with bolognese tomato sauce. A small plate of gnocchi would leave you extrememly full. A full stomach is a happy stomach! Check out the ingredients and the recipe down below! Try it out!

Ingredients

For 4 people:
  • Get a pack of gnocchi from a store which should be about 320 grams of pre made gnocchi.
  • Water
  • Basil
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • vegetables: onion, green pepper, red pepper
  • Seasoning: Paprika, Chili Powder, Salt, Pepper, Onion and Garlic Powder
